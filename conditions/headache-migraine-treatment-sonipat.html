Headache & Migraine

Reduce frequency. Lessen intensity. Regain calm.

We identify triggers, calm neck and jaw tension, and build a lifestyle routine that supports more predictable days. Youll learn exactly what helps during a flare and what keeps them away.

Common drivers we treat

Neck & jaw tension

Cervical mobility and TMJ relaxation drills reduce referred pain to the head. Acupressure helps the nervous system downshift.

Habits & environment

Screen glare, meal timing, hydration, and sleep patterns can prime flares. We make small, realistic changes that fit your life.

Stress & rhythm

Gentle breathwork, sunlight, and steady mealtimes build resilience. Ayurveda adds calming rituals that are easy to maintain.

Well also discuss red flags and when to seek medical advice, ensuring your care stays safe and comprehensive.

Your practical plan

  1. Relief kit: Dark room, hydration, neck heat, acupressure sequence, and short breathing drill.
  2. Headache hygiene: Daily micro-mobility, screen breaks, jaw relaxers, and evening routine to wind down.
  3. Long-term: Strength for neck/upper back, aerobic activity you enjoy, and a simple morning rhythm.

When to consult a physician

First-ever severe headache, sudden change in pattern, neurological symptoms, or head injury. We coordinate with your doctor when needed.

Understanding triggers

Common drivers: neck and jaw tension, screen glare, inconsistent meals, dehydration, and poor sleep. Hormonal shifts can also increase sensitivity. We prioritize a few changes that make the biggest difference quickly.

Your relief kit

  • Dark, cool room; hydrate; warm compress for neck/jaw.
  • Acupressure sequence and slow breathing for 2 minutes.
  • Short walk once symptoms settle to prevent rebound.

Headache hygiene

Daily micro-mobility, screen breaks, jaw relaxers, evening wind-down, and a predictable sleep window. Track frequency and intensity so we can tailor next steps.

Safety

First-ever severe headache, sudden change in pattern, neurological signs, or head injury: consult a physician. We coordinate care as needed.
