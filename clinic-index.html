Our method

Frequently asked questions

How many physiotherapy sessions will I need?

Acute strains often improve within four sessions when combined with a simple home routine. Longer-standing patterns may need a phased approach over six to eight weeks. We reassess regularly and keep you informed so you always know what’s next.

Is Panchakarma right for me?

Panchakarma is a structured Ayurvedic detox and rejuvenation system using preparatory care (Snehana, Swedana) and five karmas: Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana. Suitability depends on age, constitution, symptoms, and goals. We personalize protocols and pacing.

Is acupressure safe?

Acupressure is non-invasive and typically very safe when delivered by trained practitioners. We avoid contraindicated points in pregnancy, adapt techniques for seniors, and always work within your comfort.
