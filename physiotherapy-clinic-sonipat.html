Personalized physiotherapy in Sonipat for pain relief & rehabilitation

When pain limits your daily life, the right physiotherapy plan restores movement and confidence. At Healing Power Clinic in Sonipat, we combine hands-on manual therapy, targeted exercise, and simple home strategies to deliver steady progress you can feel each week.

Whether you’re dealing with back pain, neck pain, knee arthritis, sciatica, slip disc, frozen shoulder, or recovery after surgery, our approach is to understand the whole picture: how you move, sit, sleep, and work; what triggers flare-ups; and which habits support healing. Then we build a plan that fits your body and routine, not the other way around.

  • Manual therapy: joint mobilization, soft-tissue techniques, nerve glides to calm pain and improve mobility.
  • Exercise rehabilitation: progressive loading, mobility drills, stability training to build durable strength.
  • Sports physiotherapy: return-to-sport programs with movement screening and load management.
  • Neuro physiotherapy: stroke rehabilitation, balance/gait training, and functional independence.
  • Pain education: simple explanations of what’s happening and how we’ll change it together.

Who we help

We support office professionals with posture-related pain, athletes with overload injuries, seniors seeking safe mobility, and anyone wishing to move freely again. If you’ve tried rest and painkillers without lasting change, it’s time for a structured plan that builds resilience week by week.

Your first session: clear, calm, and actionable

We start with a thorough assessment: history, movement screen, joint and muscle tests, and where needed, special tests to rule out red flags. You’ll understand the drivers of your pain and a step-by-step plan to change them. We begin treatment the same day and give you one or two simple practices to do at home—not a long list.

Neuro physiotherapy

FAQs

How many sessions will I need?

Acute pain may settle in 4 sessions with consistent home practice; longer conditions may need 6–8 weeks of progressive work. We reassess regularly and guarantee transparency about progress.

Do you offer home visit physiotherapy in Sonipat?

Yes, limited home physiotherapy is available within our service radius. Please call to check availability.

Will I get exercises?

Yes—minimal, targeted, and easy to follow. Expect small daily wins that add up.

Assessment deep-dive

A great plan starts with a great assessment. We check how you move and how your symptoms behave under gentle, safe tests. For nerve-related pain, we use careful sliders and tensioners only within comfort. For joint and soft-tissue concerns, we look at mobility, strength, control, and posture habits. Then we prioritize the two or three biggest levers that will create change this week.

Back pain & sciatica roadmap

We calm irritation first—with positions of ease, manual therapy where helpful, and simple home drills. Then we rebuild hip and trunk capacity with graded loading. Education is key: you’ll learn when to push and when to pause. For sciatica, nerve health improves when movement is dosed kindly and sleep gets better—two levers we focus on.

Knee arthritis pathway

We combine activity modification, taping, and progressive strengthening for quadriceps, hips, and calves. We practice stairs, sit-to-stand, and floor transfers so life gets easier quickly. Footwear and pacing strategies reduce day-to-day spikes. Ayurveda supports weight, sleep, and digestion—quiet drivers of knee comfort.

Post-surgery rehabilitation

After ACL, meniscus, or rotator cuff procedures, we use phased protocols: swelling control, range, strength, and return-to-function. We coordinate with your surgeon’s guidance and keep you motivated with measurable milestones.

Home physiotherapy (limited radius)

When needed, we provide home visits within our service radius. We bring the same clarity and structure to your space and teach caregivers safe support techniques.
