Back Pain

Lumbar Spondylosis Treatment in Sonipat

Lower back pain is common—and highly treatable. We blend education, movement, and targeted strengthening with calming therapies to help you feel secure and capable again.

A calm, structured plan

  • Find positions of ease for quick relief and better sleep.
  • Introduce mobility within pain-free arcs; avoid forcing end ranges.
  • Build strength for hips, core, and back with graded loading.
  • Practice daily tasks safely: sit, lift, reach, and carry.

Ayurvedic routines support digestion and sleep—two quiet levers that change how the back feels day to day.

FAQs

Is spondylosis permanent?

Age-related changes are common and do not equal pain. Capacity, confidence, and habits strongly influence symptoms—and they are modifiable.

Can I keep working?

Usually yes. We plan breaks and safe techniques so you can continue while healing.

Do belts help?

Sometimes temporarily during heavier tasks. We aim to strengthen so you rely on them less over time.

Lower back relief that lasts

We combine education, mobility, and graded strength so daily tasks feel safe again. Youll learn the difference between helpful effort and unhelpful forcing, and how to pace busy weeks without setting yourself back.

Chores & daily life

We train hinge mechanics for lifting, squatting to pick up items, and safe carries. We plan breaks for desk work and driving so the back doesnt stew in one posture.

Flare strategy

Scale loads down briefly, return to positions of ease, and keep moving within comfort. Resume progressions within days as symptoms settle.

Return timeline

Many feel steady improvement within 4 6 weeks; complex cases may take longer. We keep milestones visible and adjust quickly.
