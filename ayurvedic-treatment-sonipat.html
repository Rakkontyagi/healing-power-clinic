Ayurveda in Sonipat

Holistic Ayurvedic consultations & therapies

Ayurveda meets you where you are. We assess your constitution (prakriti), current imbalances (vikriti), and lifestyle to craft a plan that feels kind and sustainablenot extreme, not confusing, just deeply practical.

Consultation & diagnosis

We begin with Nadi Pariksha (pulse assessment) alongside observation of tongue, skin, sleep, appetite, and digestion. We explore your daily rhythms and stress patterns, because routines can heal as much as herbs. After discussing goals, we share a clear plan with stagingwhat to do now, what to layer in next, and what to keep for later.

We close any gaps in safety by asking about current medications and medical history. Where needed, we coordinate with your physician and ensure our herbal suggestions are appropriate. We prefer gentle, synergistic formulas that work steadily over weeks instead of aggressive shortcuts.

Therapies & support

Shirodhara & Abhyanga

Calming therapies for the nervous system, helpful for stress, insomnia, headaches, and burnout. We use clean, warm oils and maintain quiet, soothing spaces.

Nasya & Basti

Nasya supports sinus and clarity; Basti nourishes and grounds Vata, supporting joints and digestion. Both are applied only when indicated and safely screened.

Diet & Lifestyle

We make diet practical: simple seasonal meals, gentle spices, warm water, and timing that supports digestion. Lifestyle includes breath, gentle movement, sunlight, and consistent sleep.

When combined with physiotherapy, Ayurveda helps pain settle faster by improving sleep quality, digestion, and overall recovery. For metabolic and weight concerns, we favor steady changes you can sustain.

FAQs

Are ayurvedic medicines safe?

We recommend reputable sources and avoid unnecessary polypharmacy. We watch for interactions and adjust according to your response.

How soon will I see results?

Sleep and digestion often improve within two weeks when routines align. Chronic conditions may require a staged plan over months, reviewed regularly.

Is Ayurveda compatible with modern treatment?

Yes. We respect your current medical plan, coordinate when needed, and use Ayurveda to support recovery and resilience.

Your Ayurvedic consultation flow

We start with a calm, unhurried conversation: medical history, digestion, sleep, stress, skin, joint comfort, and menstrual health where relevant. We observe tongue and skin, assess pulse (Nadi Pariksha), and understand your daily rhythm. Then we co-create goals and a phased plan so changes feel realistic from day one.

Lifestyle & meals you can actually follow

We prefer warm, seasonal, minimally processed meals and consistent timing. We design simple plates you enjoy and can maintain on busy days. Evening wind-downs—light dinner, warm water, soft lights—set sleep up for success. Breathwork and sunlight complete the rhythm.

Therapies applied judiciously

Depending on your needs, we may suggest Shirodhara for stress and sleep, Nasya for sinus and clarity, Basti for Vata imbalance, or gentle Abhyanga for nervous system calm. We screen carefully for safety and pace intensity to protect comfort.

Herbs & safety

We prescribe conservatively, using reputable sources and minimal formulations. We monitor for interactions with current medications and adjust based on response. The goal is steady, sustainable change—not quick, fragile wins.

Integration with physiotherapy

For pain and rehab, Ayurveda supports the foundations—sleep and digestion—so tissues tolerate graded loading more comfortably. Together, these modalities help results arrive faster and stick longer.
